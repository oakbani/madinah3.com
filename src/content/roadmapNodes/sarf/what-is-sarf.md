**Sarf (الصرف)** is the study of how Arabic words are *formed* — how a small set
of root letters expands into nouns, verbs, and participles by following
predictable patterns.

If *Nahw* tells you how words behave in a sentence, *Sarf* tells you where the
words themselves come from.
