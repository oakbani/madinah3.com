---
title: Hamzated verbs (المهموز)
---

A root that contains a **hamza (ء)** as one of its three letters. Sub-types
by hamza position:

- **Mahmūz al-fā'** — hamza is the first letter (e.g. *أَكَلَ* — to eat).
- **Mahmūz al-ʿayn** — hamza is the middle letter (e.g. *سَأَلَ* — to ask).
- **Mahmūz al-lām** — hamza is the last letter (e.g. *قَرَأَ* — to read).

The conjugation is mostly regular; the spelling of the hamza follows a few
extra rules.
