---
title: Pronoun conjugation
---

Each verb conjugates across **14 pronoun forms** (3 persons × singular / dual /
plural × masculine / feminine, with overlaps). For example, past tense of
**نصر**:

- **هو** نَصَرَ — he helped
- **هي** نَصَرَتْ — she helped
- **هم** نَصَرُوا — they (m.) helped
- **أنا** نَصَرْتُ — I helped
- **نحن** نَصَرْنَا — we helped
