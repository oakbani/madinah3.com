---
title: Sound verbs (الصحيح السالم)
---

The "regular" family — all three root letters are **strong** (no hamza, no
weak letter, no doubling). Conjugation follows the textbook patterns with
no surprises.

Examples: **نَصَرَ, كَتَبَ, ضَرَبَ, جَلَسَ**.

Master sound verbs first; everything else is a variation on these.
