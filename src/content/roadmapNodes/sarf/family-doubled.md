---
title: Doubled verbs (المضعّف)
---

A root whose **second and third letters are identical**, written with a
shadda. Examples:

- **مَدَّ** (root م‑د‑د) — to extend
- **رَدَّ** (root ر‑د‑د) — to return
- **شَكَّ** (root ش‑ك‑ك) — to doubt

Conjugation usually keeps the shadda, but it splits in some forms (e.g.
**مَدَدْتُ** — *I extended*).
