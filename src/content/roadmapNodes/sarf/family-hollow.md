---
title: Hollow verbs (الأجوف)
---

A root whose **middle letter is weak** (و or ي), which then surfaces as a long
**ا** in the past tense:

- **قَالَ** (root ق‑و‑ل) — to say
- **بَاعَ** (root ب‑ي‑ع) — to sell
- **خَافَ** (root خ‑و‑ف) — to fear

The middle letter shrinks or vanishes in certain conjugations
(e.g. **قُلْتُ** — *I said*).
