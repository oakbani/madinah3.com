---
title: Present tense (المضارع)
---

The present/imperfect tense (*al-muḍāriʿ*) describes an ongoing or future
action. It's built by prefixing one of **أ‑ت‑ي‑ن** to the root and adjusting
vowels:

- يَنْصُرُ — he helps / will help
- يَذْهَبُ — he goes
- يَسْمَعُ — he hears

The middle vowel often differs from the past form and is memorized per verb.
