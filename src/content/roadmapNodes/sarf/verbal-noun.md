---
title: Verbal noun (المصدر)
---

The **maṣdar** is the noun form of the action itself — what English would
express with *"-ing"* or *"to ___"*.

- نَصَرَ → **نَصْر** (helping / victory)
- كَتَبَ → **كِتَابَة** (writing)
- عَلِمَ → **عِلْم** (knowledge)

For Form I the maṣdar pattern is partly memorized; Forms II–X have predictable
maṣdar patterns that come with practice.
