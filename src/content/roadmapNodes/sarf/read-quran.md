---
title: Apply — read Qur'anic phrases
---

Open a Qur'an page and try to spot:

1. **Roots** — strip prefixes/suffixes, find the 3 core letters.
2. **Patterns** — match the shape (فَاعِل, مَفْعُول, مُسْتَفْعِل…).
3. **Tense & person** — past or present? Who is doing the action?

Even short āyāt are full of the patterns from this roadmap. Each verse you
decode reinforces the entire system.
